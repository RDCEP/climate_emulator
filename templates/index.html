Global Circulation Models, or GCMs, provide scientific estimates of future climates. They incorporate everything scientists know about climate from weather to the oceans to ice sheets. Our emulator provides a compact statistical representation of these models of future climate. We have ‘trained’ the emulator to reproduce the output of the Community Climate System Model 4 GCM experiments. The emulator is able to reproduce the climate along four â€˜Representative Concentration Pathways’ (RCPs), which are plausible trajectories of anthropogenic emissions between 2006 and 2100.
